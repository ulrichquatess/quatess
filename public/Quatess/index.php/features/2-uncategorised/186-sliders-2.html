Sliders

Written by Super User. Posted in Uncategorised

Simple Slider

This template fits with width and height of screen which provides you larg place to put your slider, you can also have HD video playing on your background of slider.

Partial View1

This template shows one complete slide plus two partial of nearby slides at a same time which it increases the interest of seeing other slides by the visitors.

Partial View2

This template shows one complete slide plus two partial of nearby slides at a same time which it increases the interest of seeing other slides by the visitors.

Partial View3

This template shows one complete slide plus three partial of nearby slides at a same time which it increases the interest of seeing other slides by the visitors.

Flow 3D Carousel

This template shows one complete slide plus Flow 3D Carousel of nearby slides at a same time which it increases the interest of seeing other slides by the visitors.

Wave 3D Carousel

This template shows one complete slide plus Wave #d Carousel of nearby slides at a same time which it increases the interest of seeing other slides by the visitors.

Laptop Display Slider

With this template put your images on a laptop and make it alive with nice effect, you will be able to add images and even videos easily, it also supports tooltips

Desktop Display Slider

With this template put your images on a display and make it alive with nice 3d effect, you will be able to add images and even videos easily, it also supports tooltips.

Horizontal Tab Slider

This template considers one tab for each slide which it shows the tabs as thumbnails, you can put html content in tabs. You can change the size of each tab easily by using css.

Vertical Tab Slider

This template considers one tab for each slide which it shows the tabs as thumbnails, you can put html content in tabs. You can change the size of each tab easily by using css.

Full Width Slider

This template shows one complete full width slider slide plus of nearby slides at a same time which it increases the interest of seeing other slides by the visitors.

Box Slider

This template shows one complete slide plus two partial of nearby slides at a same time which it increases the interest of seeing other slides by the visitors.

Simple Slider

This template fits with width and height of screen which provides you larg place to put your slider, you can also have HD video playing on your background of slider.

Image With Text Slider

This template shows one complete slide plus image with text slider of nearby slides at a same time which it increases the interest of seeing other slides by the visitors.

Content Image Slider

This template shows one complete slide plus content image slider of nearby slides at a same time which it increases the interest of seeing other slides by the visitors.
